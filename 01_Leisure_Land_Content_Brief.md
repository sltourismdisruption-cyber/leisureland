## 1. Brand Identity

### Positioning
**Leisure Land is one of the must-go experiences in Galle.** A nature-immersed jungle water park and accommodation where families and friends can experience Sri Lanka authentically — through traditional games, hands-on cultural activities, and food made from scratch.

### One-line elevator pitch
*"Sri Lanka's nature-inspired water park, 10 minutes from Galle. A full day of slides, traditional games, and authentic Sri Lankan food. From $13 per person."*

### Core brand pillars
1. **Nature-immersed** — We don't compete with nature, we blend into it
2. **Authentic Sri Lanka** — Real food, real games, real culture
3. **Family-welcoming** — For everyone, not just luxury travelers
4. **Hands-on experiences** — You don't just visit, you participate
5. **Galle basecamp** — Stay with us, explore all of Galle

### Target customer
- **Primary:** Foreign tourists (backpackers, families) visiting Galle
- **Secondary:** Local Sri Lankans (families, friends, couples)
- **Tertiary:** Corporate groups, schools, team-building

### Brand voice
- Warm, welcoming, never pretentious
- Speaks to families and friends, not luxury travelers
- Uses nature words: immersed, jungle, paddy, tree, rope, leaf, kitchen, fresh
- **Avoids:** "premium," "exclusive," "luxury," "VIP"
- **Embraces:** "real," "authentic," "from scratch," "with our own hands," "in the jungle"

### Color palette
- **Primary green:** Tropical jungle green (deep, lush canopy)
- **Secondary brown:** Tree-trunk brown / earth brown
- **Accent blue:** Sky blue (looking up through the trees)
- **Background neutral:** Warm off-white / cream
- **Pop accent:** Sun-yellow or coral (sparingly — for CTAs)

### SECTION 10 — FAQ

**Heading:** Quick Questions, Quick Answers

**Accordion format (expand on click):**

**Q: What should I bring?**
A: Swimwear, towel, sunscreen, change of clothes. Lockers available.

**Q: Is it safe for kids?**
A: Yes — kid-friendly pools, lifeguards on duty, and family slides separate from speed slides.

**Q: Do I need to book in advance?**
A: Walk-ins welcome, but WhatsApp ahead to guarantee a spot on weekends and holidays.

**Q: What time are you open?**
A: Water park is open 9 AM to 10 PM daily. The 10-foot deep pool closes at 6 PM for safety (gets too dark).

**Q: Is the food really not spicy?**
A: We have a non-spicy buffet line specifically for foreign guests, plus spicy options if you're brave.

**Q: Vegetarian, vegan, or gluten-free?**
A: All accommodated. Just message us in advance and we'll sort it.

**Q: Do you serve alcohol?**
A: Yes — but no adventure pool or activity access after drinking. Safety first. Day guests can drink after 2 PM.

**Q: Are there quiet hours for accommodation guests?**
A: Yes — no loud sounds after 10 PM. We protect the calm so everyone enjoys the nature.

**Q: What if it rains?**
A: We handle it case by case — just WhatsApp us and we'll sort you out fairly.

**Q: Can I rent a bicycle to explore?**
A: Yes — bicycle rentals are available with advance arrangement. Let us know on WhatsApp and we'll have one ready.

**Q: Is there parking?**
A: Yes, free on-site parking.

**Q: How do I book accommodation?**
A: WhatsApp us with your dates and group size. Booking direct saves you up to 30% vs OTAs.
