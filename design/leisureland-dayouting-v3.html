How your day flows.

No schedule, no rush. The day moves the way you want it to. Follow the trail:

Arrive and breathe.

Fresh fruit juice in hand, jungle all around. The air is suddenly greener.

Play.

Slides, waterfall pools, cable bridges, the tree house. Go at your own pace.

Feast.

The Sri Lankan lunch buffet. Ask for spicy or mild, both authentic.

More play.

Kotta Pora pillow fights, Tarzan jumps, rope walking, paddy swings.

Wind down.

Black tea or coffee as the light goes golden over the paddy.

Leave happy.

Or ask us about the evening session instead.

Know before you go.

A few things that keep the day safe and easy for everyone.

Bring ID

Anyone 18 or over needs one: NIC, license or passport.

Swimwear in the pools

Swimwear or silk suits only. No denim, cotton or shawls, they're unsafe on the slides.

Towels

Not included, bring your own.

Alcohol after 2 pm only

Never before or during activities. No activities after drinking, safety first.

Lifeguards on duty

All day, every day. The deep pool closes at 6:30 pm, once the light goes.

Under 18s

Come along with a parent or guardian.

Gold jewelry

Best left at home.
